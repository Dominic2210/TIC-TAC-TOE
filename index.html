WELCOME TO THE GAME!

( Player 1 : 'X'

;

Player 2 : 'O' )

Objective:

  • The goal of the game is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols (X or O) on the 3x3 grid.

    • RULES :

  • Decide which player will be X and which will be O.
  • Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) on an empty spot on the grid.
  • The first player (X) makes the initial move.
  • A player wins by having three of their symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • If the entire grid is filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
  • Players must make a move during their turn; no skipping turns is allowed.
  • Once a player makes a move, it cannot be changed.
  • Players cannot place their symbol on a position that is already occupied by their opponent's symbol or their own.
  • The game ends when a player wins or when all positions on the grid are filled (resulting in a draw).
  • If a player achieves three in a row, their victory is announced, and the game concludes.
  • After the game ends, players can choose to play again or reset the grid for a new match.
  • Sportsmanship is encouraged, and players should congratulate the winner or acknowledge a draw.
